\chapter*{Abstract}

This book presents the theoretical and practical foundations of the integration process, addressing the definitions of indefinite and definite integrals, as well as the most common techniques for solving them. It includes properties, methods such as integration by parts, substitution, and partial fractions, along with their application in physical and geometric contexts. In addition, it offers solved exercises and problems that help reinforce the acquired knowledge.

\textbf{Keywords:} integral calculus, definite integral, substitution method, partial fractions, area under the curve, antiderivative.
